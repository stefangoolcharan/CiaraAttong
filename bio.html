Biography

Ciara Attong

Ciara Marie Attong is the author of "Dark & Cold", Book One of "The Dark & Cold Series" released in 2019, and five collections of poetry "butterfly breaths" and "euphorically chaotic dystopias" (released in 2022), "Soul Strings", "The Hollow Aches" and "The Matter of Existence" (released in 2023).


Ciara is not only an author, but an active creative, visual and performing artist, as well as an entrepreneur. She has been featured in many performances in show houses and on stages locally, as well as appearing in front of cameras in commercials, short films and televised ads. She is a vocally trained singer and an experienced actor. She is a commissioned experienced visual artist in her leisure time, and an advocate for self love, mental health and animal rights.


Miss Attong has on numerous accounts, expressed that she would love most of all to inspire and motivate others through her work -in whatever way she can. This is one of her greatest desires through pursuing this path of the arts.
